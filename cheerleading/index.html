Cheerleading

"Let's get fired up and show 'em what you got!"

That chant still echoes in my head every time I step onto the mat. Cheer taught me strength in balance and grace under pressure. From long practices to championship floors, it became more than a sport; it became a language of trust and precision. As a four-time All-Academic athlete, Most Improved awardee, and CCC nominee, I have grown as both a performer and a leader. I have coached elementary, middle school, and unified teams, and helped our squad to earn the first CCC championship in school history.
